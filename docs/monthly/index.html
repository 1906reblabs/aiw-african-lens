§ What's in Every Issue

Editor's Thesis

One falsifiable argument about where the stack is heading — tracked against last month's thesis before stating a new one.

Executive Brief

5–7 items, each self-contained: what happened, why it matters, what to watch.

Signals & Metrics

The standing KPI dashboard — capital flows, compute capacity, deployment counts — with month-over-month trend.

Deep Research Essays

One or two single-topic essays, 2,500+ words each, built to reward a full read.

Engineering Frontier

Power, chips, and connectivity as one technical narrative — real units, no hand-waving.

Commercial Strategy

Market structure, capital flow interpretation, and build/buy/partner framing for operators.

Policy & Geopolitics

Regulatory trajectory plus a RAND-style map of external actors' leverage and interest.

African Builders

2–3 rigorous profiles of the people building and governing the stack — not press-release journalism.

Models, Papers & Repositories

A standing benchmark board and repository-health tracker, updated monthly rather than narrated fresh each time.

Scenarios & Forecasts

2–4 falsifiable, probability-banded scenarios, carried forward until they resolve.

Reading List

5–10 annotated items a well-read subscriber probably has not already seen.
